# About RRK Automations

## Our Story
RRK Automations was founded by S. Mahesh Kumar as a global agency. The journey began not in a corporate office, but in moments of uncertainty and determination. While many chose traditional paths, Mahesh chose to build something of his own — relying not on a formal degree, but on skills, curiosity, and an unbreakable mindset.

Starting from scratch was not easy. There were financial pressures, societal expectations, and the constant question: "Will this work?" But what kept him moving forward was belief — belief in his vision and belief in himself.

## What We Do Today
Today, RRK Automations stands as proof that success is not defined by degrees, but by dedication, skill, and the courage to start. We deliver powerful, innovative solutions in AI automation, WhatsApp chatbots, web development, and warehouse management systems to businesses worldwide.

## Service Area
Worldwide (Global Delivery). Serving businesses on all continents and time zones.
